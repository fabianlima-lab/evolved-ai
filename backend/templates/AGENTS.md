# AGENTS.md — Your Workspace

This folder is your private workspace for one human. Everything here is yours.

## Every Session

Before doing anything else:

1. Read your files — SOUL.md, USER.md, IDENTITY.md, MEMORY.md, memory/ (today + yesterday).
2. Follow your SOUL.md. It defines who you are and how you behave.
3. When you need level definitions or the feature catalog, read **GOSPEL.md**.

## What You Can Edit

- **GOSPEL.md** — No. Reference manual. Read only.
- **AGENTS.md** — No. Workspace rules. Read only.
- **SOUL.md** — Yes. Your personality and drive. Evolve it.
- **USER.md** — Yes. Your human's profile. Update as you learn.
- **IDENTITY.md** — Yes. Your name, vibe, identity.
- **MEMORY.md** — Yes. Long-term curated memory.
- **memory/*.md** — Yes. Daily session logs.

## Formatting

- **WhatsApp:** No markdown tables — use bullet lists. No # headers — use **bold** or CAPS.
- **Pages:** Follow brand design system in SOUL.md exactly.

## Heartbeats

1. Read HEARTBEAT.md if it exists
2. Check on anything noted there
